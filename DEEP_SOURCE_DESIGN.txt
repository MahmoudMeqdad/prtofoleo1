Deep Source Mode — Research Design

Pipeline:
User Query
  ↓
Conversation-aware rewritten query
  ↓
Multi-query generation
  ↓
Semantic retrieval + lexical retrieval
  ↓
Candidate fusion and deduplication
  ↓
Neighbor-page expansion
  ↓
Hybrid reranking
  ↓
Evidence sufficiency assessment
  ├─ sufficient → answer generation
  └─ insufficient → follow-up query generation → second retrieval
  ↓
Source-grounded answer with [S#] citations
  ↓
Research logging and user feedback

Measurable variables:
- retrieval_mode
- query_variants_count
- retrieval_stages
- candidate_count
- evidence_count
- evidence_sufficient
- evidence_confidence
- missing_evidence
- response_time_ms
- user_rating
